Privacy Policy for AI Image Analyzer

Last updated: January 10, 2026

Information We Collect

AI Image Analyzer does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app operates as follows:

  • Images are processed locally on your device
  • Images are sent to Google's Gemini API for analysis only
  • We do not store your photos on our servers
  • We do not track your usage or collect analytics

Photo Library Access

The app requests access to your photo library solely to allow you to select images for AI analysis. Selected images are sent directly to Google's Gemini API and are not stored by us.

Third-Party Services

This app uses Google's Gemini API for image analysis. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle data: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Security

No user data is stored on our servers. All image processing requests are transmitted securely via HTTPS.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
