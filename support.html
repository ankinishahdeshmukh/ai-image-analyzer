üìñ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I analyze an image?
Simply tap "Select Photo", choose an image from your library, and tap "Analyze". The AI will provide a description in seconds.
Is my data private?
Yes! We don't store any of your photos. Images are sent directly to Google's Gemini API for analysis and are not saved on our servers.
Why do you need photo library access?
We need access to your photo library so you can select images to analyze. We only access the specific photos you choose.
Does the app work offline?
No, the app requires an internet connection to send images to the AI analysis service.
Is the app free?
Yes, AI Image Analyzer is completely free to use.
